# Privacy Policy for AuraFit - Virtual Try-On

**Effective Date:** May 19, 2026

Welcome to AuraFit. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience when using our app.

## 1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your **Name** and **Email Address**. Your password is securely stored and never visible to anyone, including us.

When you use the try-on feature, you upload a photo of yourself and a clothing image. These are used only to generate your virtual try-on result.

## 2. How We Use Your Photos

- Your photos are **only used to generate the try-on result** — nothing else.
- We send your photos to a secure third-party AI service to process the try-on. Once the result is generated, the photos are immediately discarded.
- **We do not store your photos on our servers.**

## 3. Where Your Data is Stored

- Your try-on history and favorite images are saved **only on your phone**, not on any cloud or server.
- All saved images on your phone are **encrypted** so they stay private even if someone else accesses your device.

## 4. Who Can See Your Data

We do **not** sell, share, or give your personal information to anyone. The only exception is the AI service that processes your try-on photos, and they do not keep your images.

## 5. Deleting Your Data

You can delete your account and all your data at any time directly from the app by going to **Settings → Delete Account**. You can also clear your local history and favorites from the Settings screen.
